Dataset Information

D liu c chia thnh hai nhm:

  1. training set (train.csv)
  2. test set (test.csv)

  • Training set nn c s dng xy dng cc m hnh hc my. i vi training set, ta cung cp kt qu cho tng hnh khch. M hnh s da trn cc c im nh gii tnh v hng ca hnh khch. Ta cng c th s dng k thut tnh nng to cc tnh nng mi.

  • Test set nn c s dng xem m hnh hot ng tt nh th no trn d liu khng c nhn thy. i vi test set, ta khng cung cp thng tin c bn cho tng hnh khch. Cng vic l d on nhng kt qu ny. i vi mi hnh khch trong nhm th nghim, ta s s dng m hnh train d on liu h c sng st sau v chm tu Titanic hay khng.

  • Sex: gii tnh hnh khch.

  • Age: tui ca hnh khch. Nu tui nh hn 1 th dng s l (0.42), nu tui l c lng th dng xx.5. R rng y cng s l mt c trng tim nng d on kt qu cho bi ton v tr em v ngi gi vo nhm c nguy c cao hn.

  • Sibsp: s lng anh ch em hoc v/chng cng trn tu.

  • Parch: s lng b m/con ci cng trn tu.

  • Ticket: m s v.

  • Fare: gi v.

  • Embarked: Ni ln tu, C = Cherbourg, Q = Queenstown, S = Southampton.

Model Training¶

By gi qu trnh preprocessing c thc hin, ta s thc hin training v th nghim m hnh.

  • Nu ta chy v kim tra ton b tp d liu, kt qu s khng chnh xc. Do , ta s s dng train_test_split.

  • Ta s thm Random_state vi thuc tnh 42 c c s phn chia tng t khi chy li.

  • Nu khng ch nh trng thi ngu nhin, n s phn chia d liu mt cch ngu nhin khi chy li cho kt qu khng nht qun.

from sklearn.model_selection import train_test_split, cross_val_score
# classify column
def classify(model):
    x_train, x_test, y_train, y_test = train_test_split(X, y, test_size=0.25, random_state=42)
    model.fit(x_train, y_train)
    print('Accuracy:', model.score(x_test, y_test))
    
    score = cross_val_score(model, X, y, cv=5)
    print('CV Score:', np.mean(score))
